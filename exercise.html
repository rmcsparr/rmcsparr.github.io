Exercise Guide

This guide introduces common exercises that can help improve strength, endurance, flexibility, and overall fitness. Always focus on proper form and consistency when exercising.

Push-Ups

Push-ups strengthen the chest, shoulders, and triceps. Keep your body straight, lower yourself slowly, and push back up with control.

Squats

Squats target the legs and glutes. Keep your chest up, bend your knees, and lower your hips until your thighs are parallel to the floor.

Planks

Planks improve core strength and stability. Hold your body in a straight line while keeping your core tight and your back flat.

Jump Rope

Jump rope is a cardio exercise that improves endurance, coordination, and heart health while helping burn calories.

Exercise Safety Tips

  • Warm up before every workout.
  • Use proper form to avoid injury.
  • Stay hydrated during exercise.
  • Start slowly and increase intensity over time.
  • Rest and recover between workouts.
